    In 5 Easy Steps

    Visit the SmartBike website now

    LOCATE BIKE

    Use the SmartBike website to find the closest vehicle to you.

    SCAN QR CODE

    To unlock the vehicle, simply scan the QR code located on the panel.

    RIDE

    Enjoy your but remember to abide by all the traffic laws and parking policies.

    END
